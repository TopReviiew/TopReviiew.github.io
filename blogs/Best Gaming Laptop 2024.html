What to Look for in a Gaming Laptop in 2024

When picking a gaming laptop in 2024, the processor (CPU) is key. It affects the laptop's power and speed. Look for the latest Intel Core or AMD Ryzen processors. They have high speeds, many cores, and efficient designs for gaming. The 14th Gen Intel Core i9 or AMD Ryzen 7000 series CPUs are great picks. They give you enough power for smooth games, multitasking, and making content.

Processor

The graphics card (GPU) is vital for a gaming laptop's performance. In 2024, the NVIDIA GeForce RTX 40-series and AMD Radeon RX 7000-series GPUs are top-notch. Choose laptops with high-end GPUs like the RTX 4090 or RX 7900 XT for great frame rates and features like ray tracing and DLSS.

Graphics Card

Choosing the right display is also important for gaming laptops in 2024. Look for laptops with high-refresh-rate panels (144Hz or higher) and high resolutions (1080p, 1440p, or 4K). Features like HDR support, wide color gamut, and low response times make the visuals better.

Display

For gaming laptops in 2024, you need enough system memory (RAM) and fast storage. Aim for at least 16GB of RAM, with the option to upgrade to 32GB or more for better multitasking and gaming. Fast solid-state drives (SSDs) with large capacities are also key for quick boot times and fast app loading.
